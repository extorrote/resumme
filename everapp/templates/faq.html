Frequently Asked Questions

Buying a Property

Q: What is the process for buying a property in Puerto Vallarta?

A: The process involves several steps: finding a property, making an offer, getting a purchase agreement, securing financing (if needed), conducting inspections, and closing the deal. Working with a knowledgeable real estate agent like Ever can simplify this process for you.

Q: Can foreigners buy property in Puerto Vallarta?

A: Yes, foreigners can buy property in Mexico, including Puerto Vallarta. There are specific areas called restricted zones where foreign buyers must use a bank trust (fideicomiso) to purchase property, but this is a common and straightforward process.

Q: What costs are associated with buying a property?

A: In addition to the purchase price, buyers should budget for closing costs, which include notary fees, registration fees, and property taxes. These can typically range from 5% to 7% of the purchase price.

Selling a Property

Q: How do I determine the value of my property?

A: The value of your property can be determined through a comparative market analysis (CMA) conducted by a real estate agent. This analysis looks at similar properties that have recently sold in your area.

Q: How long does it typically take to sell a property in Puerto Vallarta?

A: The time it takes to sell a property can vary based on market conditions, pricing, and the property’s condition. On average, it can take anywhere from a few months to over a year.

Q: What should I do to prepare my property for sale?

A: To prepare your property for sale, consider making any necessary repairs, decluttering, and staging the home to make it more appealing to buyers. A real estate agent can provide specific recommendations based on your property.

Renting a Property

Q: Can I rent out my property in Puerto Vallarta?

A: Yes, many property owners in Puerto Vallarta rent out their homes either on a short-term or long-term basis. It's important to understand local regulations and potential tax implications.

Q: What are the advantages of hiring a property management company?

A: A property management company can handle tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance, and other day-to-day tasks, making it easier for you to manage your rental property, especially if you live outside the area.
